Experimental Design Project

Latin Square and BIBD Presentation

A final presentation in design of experiments covering structured layouts, blocking principles, and ANOVA-based interpretation.

Overview

This presentation introduces classical experimental designs that control nuisance variation through structured layouts and balanced treatment allocation.

Topics Covered

  • Latin square design
  • Graeco-Latin square design
  • Replication structures
  • Balanced incomplete block design
  • ANOVA interpretation and worked examples

Academic Value

The work highlights both design logic and statistical interpretation, showing how treatment effects can be evaluated while controlling for blocking factors and layout constraints.
